About Rules

Welcome to our community! We have a total of 25 rules, divided into 5 sections for clarity and structure.
Each section contains 5 specific rules that every member is expected to follow:
Please read all sections carefully and follow every rule. Adhering to these guidelines ensures a safe, fair, and enjoyable experience for everyone.

1. General Rules: Guidelines about behavior, respect, and overall community standards.
2. Safety Rules: Instructions to keep members and data secure, and avoid harmful actions.
3. Conduct Rules: How to interact with others, maintain etiquette, and promote positive engagement.
4. Content Rules: Rules about what content can be shared, posted, or discussed.
5. Participation Rules: Expectations for involvement, contributions, and responsibilities within the community.

Rules Staff

General Rules

1. Respect Everyone: Treat all members with kindness and respect. Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination will not be tolerated.

2. No Spamming: Avoid posting repetitive messages, advertisements, or irrelevant content.

3. Follow Instructions: Always follow directions from moderators and community leaders.

4. Stay On Topic: Keep discussions relevant to the community’s purpose and focus.

5. Use Appropriate Language: Avoid offensive, vulgar, or inappropriate language in all communications.

Safely Rules

1. Protect Personal Information: Do not share private information about yourself or others.

2. Report Suspicious Activity: Notify moderators if you see anything unsafe or harmful.

3. No Malicious Content: Do not post viruses, malware, or phishing links.

4. Follow Safety Protocols: Always comply with safety instructions in community tools or platforms.

5. Respect Privacy: Avoid recording or taking screenshots without consent.

Conduct Rules

1. Be Polite: Use courteous language and remain calm during disagreements.

2. No Harassment: Avoid bullying, threats, or intimidation.

3. Help New Members: Guide newcomers and answer questions respectfully.

4. Avoid Arguments: Keep discussions productive and avoid personal conflicts.

5. Follow Community Etiquette: Observe all rules of behavior and maintain professionalism.

Participation Rules

1. Be Active: Participate regularly and contribute positively to discussions.

2. Attend Required Events: Join meetings, activities, or mandatory sessions as scheduled.

3. Complete Assigned Tasks: Fulfill responsibilities assigned by moderators or leaders.

4. Collaborate: Work cooperatively with other members to achieve community goals.

5. Follow Feedback: Accept constructive feedback and improve your participation accordingly.

Content Rules

1. Share Relevant Content: Only post content related to the community’s purpose.

2. No Illegal Content: Avoid sharing copyrighted, pirated, or illegal materials.

3. Avoid Offensive Material: Do not post nudity, graphic violence, or hate content.

4. Give Credit: Always credit authors when sharing someone else’s work.

5. Maintain Accuracy: Verify information before posting to prevent misinformation.
